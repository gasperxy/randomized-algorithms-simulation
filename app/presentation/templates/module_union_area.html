Monte Carlo ε-δ intuition: we generate random rectangles, peel away overlapping portions to get disjoint \(B_i\) slices, and then estimate the union area by sampling rectangles proportional to their area and checking whether the sampled point still lies in the untouched slice.

Parameters control the number of rectangles, their size range and canvas, the number of samples, playback speed, and RNG seed for reproducibility.
