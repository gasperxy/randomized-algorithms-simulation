Papadimitriou-style randomized local search for 3-SAT:

  • Start with a random assignment.
  • Repeat up to \(3n\) steps: pick an unsatisfied 3-clause uniformly, flip one of its variables at random.
  • If all clauses are satisfied, stop; otherwise restart with a fresh assignment and repeat.

Default restarts use \(m \approx 2 r \sqrt{n} (4/3)^n\) (capped) for repeat factor \(r\); the chance of a false â€œunsatisfiedâ€ when a solution exists is bounded by \(2^{-r}\). You can override \(m\) manually.
